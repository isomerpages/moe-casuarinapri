<p>The CCA programme provides our students with the opportunities not only
to develop traits like resilience, team spirit and leadership but also
to accommodate inter-racial mixing and bonding within the school as well
as with other schools.</p>
<p>CCA offered by the school are as follows:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="/cca/sports-and-games/badminton/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Physical Sports</a>&nbsp;-
Badminton, Floorball, Football, Rugby, Wushu.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/cca/visual-and-performing-arts/art-and-crafts/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Visual and Performing Arts</a>&nbsp;-
Art and Crafts, Concert Band, Chinese Dance, Choir, Modern Dance, Malay
Dance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/cca/uniformed-groups/brownies/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Uniformed Groups</a>&nbsp;-
Brownies, Scouts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/cca/clubs-and-societies/infocomm-technology/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Clubs and Societies</a>&nbsp;-
InfoComm Technology, Environmental Science.</p>
</li>
</ul>
