      <td style="padding: 6px 8px; border-top: 1px solid #eee; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
The Art and Crafts Club offers students hands-on opportunities to explore a range of visual art forms, with ceramics as a key area of practice. Through working with different materials and media, students learn to develop ideas into meaningful artworks through thoughtful design and craftsmanship.<br><br>
Regular art-making sessions help students build confidence, patience, and perseverance, while opportunities to showcase their works at events such as the CCA Experience, Singapore Youth Festival Arts Exhibition, and school exhibitions allow them to share their learning. Through collaborative projects and shared responsibilities, students also develop communication skills, respect for diverse perspectives, and a strong sense of belonging.
