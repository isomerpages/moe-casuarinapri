<p>The Art &amp; Crafts CCA provides the platform for students to create
and appreciate the arts. Students will be exposed to different art skills
as they create their own art pieces.</p>
<p>Besides creating art pieces and learning within the school context, students
will have the opportunity to learn beyond school. The learning journeys
to National Gallery and Singapore Art Museum provide a wider&nbsp; experience
for our Art &amp; Crafts CCA members to understand and appreciate the different
forms of art.</p>
<p>In 2023, the Art &amp; Crafts CCA took part in the Singapore Youth Festival
Arts Exhibition.</p>
