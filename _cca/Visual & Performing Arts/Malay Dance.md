      <td style="padding: 6px 8px; border-top: 1px solid #eee; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
The Malay Dance CCA promotes cultural appreciation, discipline, and teamwork through the learning and practice of traditional Malay dance. Members are guided to express themselves with grace and confidence while developing respect for cultural heritage, commitment, and collaboration. Through regular training and performances, students gain a deeper understanding of the values and traditions rooted in Malay culture.<br><br>
The group’s dedication and artistic excellence were recognised when they attained the Certificate of Distinction at the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation in 2024. Members also contribute to school productions such as the school musical, enriching the school community through vibrant performances that celebrate cultural diversity and unity.
