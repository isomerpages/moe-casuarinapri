      <td style="padding: 6px 8px; border-top: 1px solid #eee; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
The Modern Dance CCA exposes students to contemporary and hip hop dance, providing a dynamic platform for creative expression and storytelling through movement. Through regular training and performances, students build confidence, resilience and perseverance, while showcasing their talents at events such as the CCA Experience, Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation, school musicals and school-based performances.<br><br>
The programme also emphasises teamwork and collaboration, as students learn to synchronise movements, support one another and develop strong communication skills and camaraderie beyond the dance studio.
