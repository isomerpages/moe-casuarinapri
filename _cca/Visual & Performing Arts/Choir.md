      <td style="padding: 6px 8px; border-top: 1px solid #eee; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
The School Choir provides a nurturing community where students develop confidence, character, and a love for music through singing together. Choristers build self-assurance, form meaningful friendships, and experience the joy of making music as a team.<br><br>
Through regular rehearsals and performances, students develop musical skills, focus, discipline, and teamwork. The choir offers opportunities to perform at major events such as the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation, as well as school celebrations including National Day and the annual award ceremony.<br><br>
Beyond the school, choristers give back to the community through outreach performances for the elderly, using music to spread joy while learning compassion and social responsibility. Through these experiences, the choir strengthens community ties and creates meaningful memories for its members.
