<p>The Concert Band provides the platform for students to learn a variety
of wind, brass and percussion instruments. These include trumpet, clarinet,
horn, xylophones and drums.</p>
<p>Our members learn to appreciate various music genres and work as a team
to perform and create wonderful music. Through these activities, our students
develop the 21st Century Competencies such as collaboration, teamwork and
leadership.</p>
<p>Our Concert Band members performed at various platforms namely school-based
and public performances such as National Day Observance Celebration and
Annual Award Ceremony.</p>
<p>In 2022, the Concert Band CCA participated in the Singapore Youth Festival
(SYF) Presentation.</p>
<p>This year, the Concert Band CCA will be taking part in the SYF 2024.</p>
