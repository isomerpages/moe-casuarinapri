<p>Football CCA provides the platform for students to develop their set skills
through drills and modified games. Apart from honing their skills, students
learn the importance of teamwork and the values of perseverance, resilience
as well as respect.</p>
<p>Our students will have the opportunity to participate in The National
School Games Football Championships Senior and Junior Divisions and other
competitions.</p>
