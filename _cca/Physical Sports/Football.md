The Football CCA provides students with opportunities to develop fundamental football skills, teamwork, and discipline through structured and regular training. Conducted under the guidance of qualified instructor, training sessions focus on technical proficiency, tactical awareness, and safe play.
<br><br>
Through participation, students cultivate important values such as perseverance, sportsmanship, leadership, and self-discipline. Members are also given opportunities to represent the school in the National School Games Football Championships (Junior and Senior Divisions), as well as in friendly matches and other external competitions, enabling them to build confidence and gain valuable competitive experience.
