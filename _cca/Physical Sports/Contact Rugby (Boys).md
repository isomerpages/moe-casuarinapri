      <td style="padding: 6px 8px; border-top: 1px solid #eee; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee;">
The Rugby CCA aims to develop students holistically by instilling core values such as sportsmanship, discipline, teamwork, integrity, leadership, and physical and mental resilience. Beyond acquiring rugby skills, students are guided to care for one another, demonstrate respect on and off the field, and build confidence through teamwork and perseverance.<br><br>
More than a contact sport, the CCA emphasizes character development through structured training and match experiences. Students learn responsibility, self-control, and the importance of supporting their teammates in both success and challenge.<br><br>
The Contact Rugby CCA is open to Primary 3 to Primary 6 students who demonstrate interest, commitment, and a willingness to learn. Selected students will have opportunities to represent the school in the National School Games Rugby Championships (Junior and Senior Divisions).
