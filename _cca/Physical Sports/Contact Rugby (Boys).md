<p>Contact Rugby CCA aims to instill the values of Sportsmanship, Discipline,
Teamwork, Integrity, Leadership skills, Physical and Mental resilience.
It is about discipline, teamwork and caring for one another on and off
the field. This CCA definitely offers much more beyond the contact rugby
skills.</p>
<p>The CCA is open to Primary 3 to Primary 6 students who have the interest
and commitment in the game.</p>
<p>Our Rugby boys will have the opportunity to participate in The National
School Games Rugby Championships Senior and Junior Divisions.</p>
