<p>At Infocomm Technology CCA, students are provided with varied activities
and programmes to hone and deepen their knowledge and skills in information,
communication and technology.</p>
<p>Students are given access to the latest technology to express their thoughts
and ideas into visual forms. Students are given the opportunity to create
a video based on a storyboard. This includes photography skills, video
editing skills and post production tools.</p>
<p>In addition, students participate in interesting programmmes and activities
such as coding and using an online website to create 3D objects.</p>
